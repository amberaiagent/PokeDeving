PokeDungeon — creature sprites
==============================

Put a CC0 (or commercial-OK) monster sprite-sheet PNG here, named:

    monsters.png

i.e. the full path:  public/assets/monsters.png

Recommended free packs (check the license says CC0 / commercial use):
  • https://itch.io/game-assets/assets-cc0/tag-monsters
  • https://itch.io/game-assets/free/tag-monsters/tag-pixel-art
  • https://opengameart.org/content/pixel-art-rpg-monster-sprites

What I need from the sheet:
  • at least 10 distinct creature frames
  • a regular grid (all frames the same size, e.g. 16x16 / 32x32 / 48x48)

Once monsters.png is in this folder, tell me — I'll open the image,
read its grid, and wire each frame to a creature in game.js (the SHEET
config). Until then the game uses the built-in drawn creatures, so
nothing breaks.
