WHAT IS POKERIPPING

$RIPPING is a token on pump.fun. Every trade generates fees — and instead of sitting idle, those fees go to work. Our bot claims them, spends them on real Pokémon card packs at Collector Crypt, opens the packs, and airdrops what it pulls straight to the wallets of $RIPPING holders. The better the pull, the deeper into the holder leaderboard it can land.

  1. 01

    Trade

    $RIPPING trades on pump.fun and accrues fees.

  2. 02

    Claim

    The bot automatically claims those creator fees.

  3. 03

    Rip

    Fees buy Pokémon packs on Collector Crypt — and rip them.

  4. 04

    Airdrop

    Pulled cards are airdropped to $RIPPING holders.
