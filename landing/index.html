The
loop

$RIPPING is a token on pump.fun. Every trade generates fees — and instead of sitting idle, those fees go to work. The bot claims them, buys real Pokémon card packs at Collector Crypt, opens them, and airdrops the pulls straight to holder wallets. The better the pull, the deeper into the leaderboard it can land.

  1. 01

    Trade

    $RIPPING trades on pump.fun and accrues creator fees.

  2. 02

    Claim

    The bot automatically claims those fees on-chain.

  3. 03

    Rip

    Fees buy Pokémon packs on Collector Crypt — and rip them.

  4. 04

    Airdrop

    Pulled cards are airdropped straight to $RIPPING holders.
